About Us

Studio History: Bomvan Studio was founded in 2022 with a passion for capturing life's most precious moments. Over the years, we have grown into a full-service studio offering a wide range of services from photography and videography to design and printing. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Mission: Our mission is to provide exceptional visual storytelling services that capture the essence of our clients' most important moments. We strive to deliver high-quality products and services that exceed our clients' expectations and create lasting memories.

Vision: Our vision is to be the leading provider of visual storytelling services in the region, known for our creativity, innovation, and dedication to excellence. We aim to continuously evolve and expand our services to meet the changing needs of our clients and the industry.

Team Members: Our team is comprised of talented and experienced professionals who are passionate about their craft. From photographers and videographers to designers and editors, each team member brings a unique set of skills and expertise to every project. We work collaboratively to ensure that every client receives personalized service and a product that reflects their vision and style.

Our Services

Photography

At Bomvan Studio, we have experienced and skilled photographers who use high-quality equipment and techniques to capture stunning images. We offer services such as event photography, studio photography, photo editing, printing, and album creation. We also provide clients with high-quality images that capture their special moments in a visually appealing and professional manner. Our goal is to create images that tell the story of your event in a way that is meaningful and timeless.

Videography

We offer a wide range of services to cater to your needs, including pre-event consultations, multi-camera coverage, highlight reels, full-length videos, customized editing, and delivery in your preferred format. Our team of experienced videographers captures every detail of the event, from the smallest moments to the biggest highlights. Whether you're planning a wedding, a corporate event, a concert, or any other special occasion, we're here to help you create a video that you'll cherish for years to come.

Documentary

At Bomvan Studio, we conceptualize, produce, and execute full-service company documentaries according to their needs. We pride ourselves in crafting films that captivate audiences, driving them towards responding to our client’s call to action and delivering results. Whether you're looking to document your personal journey, share your organization's mission, or highlight a special event or cause, our team will be there to ensure that your story is told in the best possible way.

Photo Framing

We provide a huge choice of award-winning photo frame options, all lovingly hand-made here in Rwamagana at our custom-built workshop. Our dedication to providing the finest picture frames starts with sourcing the best, sustainable frame moldings and combining them with durable mount boards to provide the perfect framing options for our client’s needs. We offer a wide range of framing options, from classic and elegant to modern and edgy, to suit your individual taste and preferences.

Live Streaming

You can get the freedom to live stream to your social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, or even Zoom.
